Rental Agreement

Please read and accept the terms of our rental agreement before proceeding with your reservation.

  • A $50 security deposit is required to confirm reservations.
  • A valid driver's license is required at the time of rental.
  • No one under the age of 12 is allowed to ride on the scooters.
  • Fuel charge is not included.
  • Unlimited mileage.
  • Liability insurance is included.
  • Damage insurance is available for purchase.
  • Free delivery for 2+ day rentals to Cozumel hotels or Airbnbs.
  • Payment options: Google Pay, Apple Pay, cash, credit card, debit card, or Venmo.
  • Rentals can be returned to either of our physical locations.
