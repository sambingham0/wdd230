Local Events

Stay tuned for upcoming events!

  • Harvest Festival and Craft Fair

    Celebrate the beauty of autumn at our Harvest Festival! Join us for a day filled with local artisan crafts, delicious seasonal foods, and fun activities for the whole family. Enjoy hayrides, pumpkin picking, and live entertainment throughout the day. Don’t miss out on the chance to support our local vendors and enjoy the festive spirit!

  • Halloween Parade and Costume Contest

    Come and showcase your best Halloween costume at our annual parade. Prizes will be awarded for the best costumes, and there will be plenty of treats for everyone. The parade starts at 4:00 p.m. on Main Street. Join us for a spooky evening filled with fun activities, live entertainment, and community spirit. Bring your family and friends for a night to remember!

  • Country Rodeo and Chili Cook-off

    Saddle up for a day of fun at the Country Rodeo! Watch rodeo events and taste some of the best chili in town. The cook-off begins at 11:00 a.m., with rodeo events running throughout the afternoon. Enjoy live music, cowboy games, and family-friendly activities throughout the day. It’s a great opportunity to enjoy the outdoors and taste delicious food!

Site Purpose

The purpose of the website is to promote local businesses and offer networking opportunities through events, partnerships, and resources. It also aims to provide information about San Diego's economic development, attractions, and community engagement to attract tourists and new residents.
